Israel Folau

Early Life

Irael Folau was born on the 3 of April 1989 in Minto, Sydney, NSW. His background is tongan through his two parents. Israel went to Lurnea Public School. He then moved to Westfield Sports High School before moving to Brisbane with his family. In Brisbane he went to Masden State High School where he was selected to play for the Queensland Scoolboy's at 15 and then Australian Schoolboy's team in 2006. At Australian Championships Folau was noticed by a scout and was invited to play with the Norths Devils playing in the Queensland Cup. From there he won a premiership with the Norths and became the first person to represent Queensland Under 19's while still only 16. Two years later Folau was chosen to play for the Australian Scoolboy's Team to tour Wales, England and France. At the end of the tour Irael was awarded the Australian Secondary Scool Rugby Leagues (ASSRL) Award for best back of the tornament. Melbourne confident enough in him to offer him contract to play in the NRL.
